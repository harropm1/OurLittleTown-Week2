Our Little Town

Welcome to Stars Hollow!

Welcome to our little corner of the world! We're glad you're here. We hope we can make your stay as fun as possible. Here you can book reservations at the Dragonfly Inn, set up a car reservation at ACME Car Rentals, or look for activities in the area! Hope to see you soon!

  • Dragonfly Inn Reservations

    Looking to book a room? The Dragonfly Inn is the only hotel in town, but it is a wonderfully restored to it's former glory, and the owners, Lorelai and Sookie, will go out of their way to make sure your stay is perfect.

    Book your stay

  • ACME Car Rentals

    Interested in renting a car while visiting Stars Hollow? You can pick one up from ACME Car Rentals, conveniently located on Main Street, like almost everything else. From here, you can take excursions to local attractions, including the city and local hiking trails.

    Start your drive

  • Things to Do

    Besides exploring our town and the shops and restaurants here, there are many local attractions including Invisible Falls, ziplining in the Canyons, and many museums and galleries. Click here to get started planning even more adventures!
